1. Check if you Qualify

Every member of our Guild is a respected member of the community of film reviewers of long standing. We expect you are no less. Read our requirements for membership to the Guild below and our expectations. Pay particular attention to the length of service and volume of reviews.

2. Find a referer

Membership requires a referral from an existing member recommending your request. You can browse the list of our members and choose to request a person of your choice to recommend you. That member will confirm that you fulfill the membership and ethical criteria.

3. Send us your info

Send us a reasonably structured email with information supporting your request to this address. We are particularly interested to know that you fulfill the age, residency, work experience publication output and that you agree with our expectations of ethical behaviour.
