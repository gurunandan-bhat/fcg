FCG Member, Subha J Rao received two Laadli Media Awards — Regional and National — in the Web (Op-Ed) category for [her opinion piece](https://www.thenewsminute.com/flix/opinion-vijay-sethupathi-film-maharaja-is-an-example-of-how-not-to-depict-violence-on-screen) in The News Minute on the film Maharaja and its problematic portrayal of gender violence. The film, directed by Nithilan Saminathan, stars Vijay Sethupathi and Anurag Kashyap.

The Laadli Media Awards, an initiative by Population First and supported by the United Nations Population Fund (UNFPA), honour excellence in gender-sensitive reporting, writing, and advertising. The 15th edition of the awards was held on November 19, 2025 at the NCPA in Mumbai, recognising work across both regional and national categories.
